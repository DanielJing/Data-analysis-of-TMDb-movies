Conclusions¶

Now, we can draw our conclusion that the revenue of movies from the¶

dataset is highly correlated to budget and popularity, but not so much¶

with runtime.¶

Vote_count has positive correlation with profitability as well.¶

Due to the limitation of the sample size of the dataset, we cannot say¶

this is fully represented the situation of the population.¶

Since some of the varibles are categorical one, so only descriptive statistics¶

are implemented but not inferential statistics.¶
